Travel to France

List of cities to visit:

  • Paris

    Paris is one of the most beautiful cities in the world.
    It is known worldwide for the Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame cathedral,
    and the Eiffel tower    . It has a reputation of being a romantic
    and cultural city. The city is also known for its high-quality gastronomy
    and the terraces of its cafés

  • Marseille

    Marseille is famous for its Bonne-mère, its Vieux-Port and its Château d'If.
    It is also known for its significant cultural outputs, from savon de Marseille
    to tarot, as well as its typically Southern culture of pastis and pétanque

  • Lyon

    Lyon, also spelled Lyons, capital of both the Rhône département and the
    Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes région, east-central France, set on a hilly site at
    the confluence of the Rhône and Saône rivers. It is the third largest
    city in France, after Paris and Marseille.

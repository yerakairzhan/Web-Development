Recipe of Chicken & Biscuits Bake

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
butter 1
onion 1
chicken broth 3
salt, pepper 2
frozen mixed vegetable 450g

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350˚F (180˚C).
Step 2: In a large pot over medium-high heat, melt the butter.
Step 3: Add the onion, stir until softened. About 1 minute.
Step 4: Whisk in the flour, stirring constantly to prevent the flour from browning. About 1 minute
Step 5: Whisk in the heavy cream. Bring to a light simmer until the sauce has thickened slightly. Test for additional salt/pepper.
Step 6: Add the mixed vegetables and shredded chicken, stir until fully incorporated.

Taco Soup

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
ground beef 454g
onion 1
tomato sauce 225g
tablespoons taco 2
diced tomato 450g

Step 1: Add the ground beef and onion to a large pot and stir until the beef is cooked through.
Step 2: Drain excess liquid.
Step 3: Add the kidney beans, black beans, corn, tomatoes, tomato sauce, and taco seasoning to the pot, and stir until combined.
Step 4: Cook over medium heat for 10 minutes.
Step 5: Serve with cheddar cheese, sour cream, pico de gallo, avocado, corn chips, lime wedges, and cilantro.
Step 6: Enjoy!
